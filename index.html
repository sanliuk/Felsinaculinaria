Welcome to Our Bolognese Culinary Journey

About Us

Nothing brings me greater joy than sharing the dishes I hold close to my heart. Yet, as my children have ventured off to explore the world, I've found myself with an empty kitchen and no one to pamper with my culinary creations. But today, I'm thrilled to welcome you to a unique experience. Drawing from the cherished recipes passed down through generations by my grandmother, I'm here to teach you the time-honoured secrets of Bolognese cuisine.

Why Choose Our Bolognese Cuisine Courses
Authentic Heritage

Our courses are steeped in the authentic traditions of Bolognese cooking, ensuring you learn from the very best.

Hands-On Experience

You won't just watch; you'll roll up your sleeves and create these dishes yourself, guided by our expertise.

Personalised Attention

Unlike larger culinary schools, we keep our class sizes small to provide you with personalised attention. This means you'll have ample opportunity to ask questions, seek guidance, and receive individualised feedback as you hone your culinary skills.

Ingredient Mastery

We believe that the key to truly mastering Bolognese cuisine lies in understanding and selecting the finest ingredients.

Cooking is the language everyone understands. Come learn, cook, and connect with us

Tagliatelle

€60 In person

€40 Online

Description

Long, flat ribbons of pasta, similar in shape to fettuccine but thinner. Their name, which means "silly cuts" in Italian, references the distinctive hand-cut appearance. Tagliatelle pairs wonderfully with various sauces (regular/wild boar ragù, porcini mushrooms, truffles, etc.) making it a versatile choice for Bolognese pasta dishes.
